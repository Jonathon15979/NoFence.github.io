Our Purpose

The purpose of No Fences Okinawa is to break down the barriers that exist between the Okinawan and United States military communities by presenting high school students with an environment outside of the more regimented structure of school to have an enjoyable and relaxed environment where students can interact and talk with one another. With this approach, students can talk and learn about one another without feeling forced, and hopefully discover that they have more in common than different.

What No Fences does

No Fences brings the Okinawan high school students to interact or to have a language, cultural exchange without having to pay money for it with the Us Military high school students. This also gives a chance for the students to make friends and understand one another’s culture to increase an understanding of one another

How we do it

Meetup at the specified place and get to know each other by having some ice breaker before the main event starts to let people know one other and Japanese speakers introduce themselves in English and English speakers introduce themselves in Japanese.
